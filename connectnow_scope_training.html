Defining scope
What is ConnectNow designed to do?
  1. Meet the needs of students along a wide continuum who are experiencing enough distress that they need to talk with someone immediately
  2. Sessions have no set length — focused on communicating compassion, support, and engaging the student in problem-solving and resource building
  3. Encourage students to access additional Mantra services through The Hub when available (self-care skills, therapy, Togetherall, Single Stop)
  4. Overarching goal: support, stabilization, and risk management
  5. Different from therapy — focused on here and now to help the student feel supported, stay safe, and identify next steps
Interventions
What interventions are provided in ConnectNow sessions?
  1. Empathic validation and emotional support
  2. Psychoeducation
  3. Coping skill development or guided coping skills
  4. Resource access
  5. Harm reduction
  6. Crisis management
  7. Talk through problem-solving or decision making
